TRANSPORT FACILITY:

Our college provides transport facilities to college. VVIT operates a fleet of 67 buses to provide a smooth glitch less transportation for the students and staff.

From every important place in Guntur, Vijayawada, Mangalagiri, Tenali, Ponnur, Amaravathi, Tadikonda, Kantheru, Perecherla, Nuthakki and Pedhanandhipadu.

Everyday there will be 2 trips at 4:00pm and 5:00pm respectively.Similarlly buses will come to pick you at your respective stops in 2 trips.

Own vehicals such as two-wheelers and four-wheelers are prohibited for students.Every Student must attend to college in college buses only.

It is promised that every student will reach their destinations 'safely'.
